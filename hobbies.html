Favorite Hobbies

I am an avid reader who enjoys escaping into a story. It fun being engrossed in the lives of various characters. Sometimes they “almost” feel like friends. I read a variety of books and I’m in a book club. I’m also a dancer and regularly enjoy Zumba classes with friends. I also enjoy the challenge of a large jigsaw puzzle and usually have one in progress on my coffee table.
